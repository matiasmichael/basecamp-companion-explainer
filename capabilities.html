Current Limitations

Transparency matters. Here's what your companion can't do yet — and how to work around it.

🚫

No Real-Time Audio/Video

Can't join Zoom/Meet calls or process live audio streams. Workaround: Provide meeting transcripts or notes for processing.

🚫

No Physical World Actions

Can't print, ship packages, or interact with physical devices. Workaround: Can order online, trigger smart home APIs, or send instructions to humans.

🚫

No Financial Transactions (by default)

Won't make purchases or move money without explicit authorization. Enable: Configure specific financial integrations with guardrails.

🚫

Token Window Limits

Very long conversations cause context drift. Workaround: Start fresh sessions for new topics; the file system preserves continuity.

🔑 Enabling New Capabilities

Most "can't do" items are configuration — not fundamental limits. Need a new integration? Ask your companion to set it up, or browse the Skills marketplace for 700+ connectors.
