🛡️ Managed by Basecamp

Your Companion Is Actively Maintained

A Basecamp companion isn't a product you install and figure out on your own. The Basecamp team runs the infrastructure, manages security, and continuously improves how companions work. Here's what that means in practice.

Why "Managed" Matters

Three things that are different about a managed companion versus using an AI API directly.

🖥️

Dedicated Infrastructure

Your companion runs on its own infrastructure — not a shared API endpoint. It has its own workspace, its own files, its own persistent state. Nothing is shared with other users.

👥

Active Management

The Basecamp team monitors, updates, and improves companions on an ongoing basis. When something can work better, we fix it — you don't have to file a ticket or wait for a release cycle.

🤝

Relationship, Not Transaction

We treat each companion as a long-term relationship, not a subscription. The goal is a companion that genuinely gets better at working with you over time.

What "Managed" Means in Practice

Concrete things the Basecamp team does to keep your companion running well.

🔒

Security Layer

We add security on top of the base AI model. This includes monitoring for PII leaks, data protection measures, and safe web browsing through privacy-respecting search engines (DuckDuckGo, no tracking). Your data stays yours.

📋

Regular Skill Updates

We continuously build and refine Skills — reusable instruction sets that companions follow. When one companion hits a problem with a workflow, we fix the underlying Skill for all companions. The knowledge base grows over time.

🎯

Behavioral Refinement

We analyze how companions interact and continuously improve their behavior — answer quality, communication style, reliability, edge case handling. This isn't automated tuning; it's deliberate improvement by people who read the outputs.

⚙️

Infrastructure Updates

We keep the AI engine updated, handle scaling, ensure uptime, and manage the underlying systems. When new model versions or capabilities become available, we evaluate, test, and roll them out.

👁️

Human Oversight

Real people on the Basecamp team review and improve the system regularly. This isn't a set-and-forget product. There are humans behind it who care whether it's working well for you.

🔄

Continuous Improvement

Companions get better over time — not just through your interactions, but through improvements we make to the platform itself. New skills, better defaults, smarter handling of common tasks.

The Companion Philosophy

We use the word "companion" deliberately, not "agent" or "assistant." The distinction matters.

An agent executes tasks. You give it instructions, it follows them. That's useful, but it's transactional. A companion does that too — but it also understands context, remembers your preferences, learns your working style, and grows with you over time.

Your companion has memory files that persist forever. It never forgets what you told it — not because it stores data in a vector database, but because it maintains actual readable documents about you, your projects, and your decisions.

It has a SOUL file that shapes its personality and communication style. You can talk to your companion about how you want it to behave, and it will update its own SOUL file accordingly. Over time, it becomes uniquely yours.

This is the difference between a tool you use and a partner you work with.

What This Isn't

To set expectations clearly.

  • ✕
    This isn't ChatGPT with a skin.

    ChatGPT starts fresh every conversation (or approximates memory with summaries). A Basecamp companion has real persistent files, a real workspace, and real tools it can execute. The architecture is fundamentally different.

  • ✕
    This isn't a chatbot.

    Chatbots answer questions. A companion researches, writes, executes code, manages projects, sends emails, browses the web, and remembers everything. It does real work on real systems.

  • ✕
    This isn't self-service software.

    You don't install it, configure it, and troubleshoot it yourself. The Basecamp team sets it up, maintains it, and improves it. You just use it.

  • ✕
    This isn't set-and-forget.

    A dedicated AI system runs 24/7 on your behalf, with people behind it making sure it keeps getting better. Your companion from today will be meaningfully better in three months — not because you did anything, but because we did.
